The Problem

Every study tool has the same fatal flaw.

They optimise for information delivery. Lectures move at one pace for everyone. Generic AI tools give you answers without checking if you actually understood anything. Past papers, the single most predictive preparation tool, are scattered across WhatsApp groups and disorganised portals. Access is luck, not merit.

No existing tool connects what you just learned directly to what you will be tested on.

Coast does.

How It Works

One loop. Five steps. Genuine mastery.

Coast is not a collection of features. It is a complete learning system where every step builds toward the one thing that matters: your exam.

01

Upload your lecture

Drop in your lecture slides or notes. Coast instantly transforms them into a structured lesson, building the conceptual story behind each topic with your professor's actual diagrams and visual content woven directly in. You learn with the same visual language your exam was designed around.

02

Learn with context

Before any question is asked, Coast gives you the why before the what. Every topic is introduced with narrative and context so nothing is memorised without being understood. Exercises built directly from your lecture content test the exact methods and logic your course requires.

03

Pedro ensures you understand

Pedro is your personalised Socratic AI tutor. He never gives you the answer. He asks the right questions until you find it yourself. Pedro maintains a persistent memory of your strengths, weaknesses, and learning patterns, updated every five messages. Every session builds on the last.

04

Real past exam questions, automatically matched

Once you have mastered the building blocks, Coast serves you real past examination questions mapped to exactly what you just learned. Not generic AI-generated quizzes. Verified past papers from your actual university, tagged by topic and difficulty. The exam is the ground truth.

05

Next topic unlocks

Mastery verified. Progress earned. The next module opens when you are genuinely ready, not just when time runs out.

Meet Pedro

Pedro never gives you the answer. That's the point.

Most AI tools solve your problem for you. Pedro teaches you to solve it yourself.

Pedro is a Socratic AI tutor with a three-layer memory model. Permanent notes capture stable patterns in your understanding that persist across every session. Active notes update in real time during your current session. Pattern notes track recurring misconceptions as they emerge.

Every five messages, Pedro updates his model of you. He goes deeper where you struggle. He moves faster where you are confident. He adapts to how you think, not how the average student thinks.

This is not ChatGPT with a different interface. This is a tutor that knows you.
