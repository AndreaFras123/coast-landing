Access & Pricing

Is Coast free to use?

Yes! We are launching with a generous free tier that gives you access to past papers and daily AI tutoring. We will have a Pro plan for unlimited AI deep-dives and advanced analytics, but our goal is to keep it student-budget friendly.

When can I actually start using it?

We are currently in private beta. If you join the waitlist today, you'll be first in line when we open up the next wave of invites (and you might snag some early-adopter perks).

The AI & Accuracy

Is the AI Tutor just ChatGPT wrapped in a different skin?

No. Unlike general chatbots, our AI is grounded in real, structured past paper data. It doesn't just guess; it uses the specific context of your university's curriculum and marking schemes to explain the logic behind the answer, not just the result.

Can I trust the explanations?

Our system uses a "Teacher-in-the-Loop" verification method. While AI is powerful, we verify our core datasets against official marking schemes to ensure you aren't learning the wrong thing.

Content & Subjects

Does this cover my specific university and course?

We are launching initially with International Buisness modules at Maastricht. We are adding new courses every week based on user votes, so sign up and tell us what you study!

How do you calculate the "Difficulty Rating" for questions?

We use a proprietary OCR system that analyzes the complexity of the question, the average time students take to solve it, and historical pass rates for that specific topic.

Gamification & Rewards

What exactly are "Lootboxes"?

Lootboxes are rewards you earn by maintaining study streaks and mastering topics, they come in the form of fish and bait. They contain XP boosts, customization items for your profile/avatar, and exclusive badges to show off on the leaderboard.

Can I pay to win?

Never. Your leaderboard rank is determined purely by your academic progress and mastery. You can't buy grades, and you can't buy your way to the top of our leaderboard.
